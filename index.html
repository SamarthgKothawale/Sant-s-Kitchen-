ABOUT US:

Sant's Kitchen is a modern yet homely restaurant located in the heart of Pune, known for its delicious food and warm hospitality. Founded with a passion for authentic flavors, Sant's Kitchen brings together traditional recipes and contemporary taste. The restaurant focuses on providing fresh, hygienic, and high-quality meals that feel like home-cooked food but with a restaurant-style experience.
